Our Mission

The AIE mission is to globalize and democratize higher education in the field of Inter, Diploma and Engineering by reaching the doorsteps of the learners. The AIE is committed to provide quality education across the globe through distance learning by equipping the students with the latest global developments. Our self study approach involves online technology through downloadable tools and self study material which is cost effective and beneficial for the working professionals/ entrepreneurs, who wish to seek higher education but cannot afford full time management programs due to financial or any other constraints.
